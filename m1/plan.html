Mission Statement

To keep up with the market trend and increase the over all sales of the Music centre by improving user experience and facilitating users by using an online platform

Success Evaluation

The success of this web project can be defined using below parameters:

  • Profit Increase to Expense Increase ratio
  • Increase in number of daily visitors
  • Increase in ticket sales
  • Increase in tickets booked online

Target Audience

The targeted audience for this website are specific the people from age group 18-30. This age group contains people with more affection towards modern music and events.

The website design will be created mobile friendly when its ready. It will be providing easy online ticketing as preferred by the youngsters today. This will attract them to buy tickets just by browsing on the mobile phones.

Content

  • Home: It will contain the details about the music centre. The address, useful website links and contact details
  • Events: It will contain list of events that are upcoming and on-going. The same page might provide buy ticket option.
  • Bulletin Board: This page will usually contain some updates from the music world like the launching of new music, musical concerts and other such events in the past
  • Musos: A page dedicated to music lovers and music experts
  • Members: Dedicated page for our members to login and gain further access to the website content
